Pizza is a flat, round bread that is typically topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various other ingredients, and then baked in an oven. It is a popular dish of Italian origin, particularly from Naples. In Mangalore, there are numerous pizza outlets, including well-known chains like Domino's and Pizza Hut, as well as local favorites like Pizza Valley and Redrocks Bourn Bon.
